FoodStop?

FoodStop looks to bring food to your doorstep through a peer-to-peer college campus based delivery system geared towards enhancing efficiency and fostering trust.

In the Spring of 2016, FoodStop officially launched its services, teaming up with Jillian and Joshua Moskites to offer Whey Station delivery to all parts of the Wesleyan campus for three weekends.

FoodStop plans to return Fall 2016 with an expanded arsenal of delivery options, including, but not limited to, locations such as Summerfields and WesWings.

We look forward to seeing you in the fall.
