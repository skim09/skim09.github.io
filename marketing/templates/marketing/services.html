Ending Whey Delivery

Dear Wesleyan,

Thank you very much for being a part of our trial run over these past 3 weeks; your support has been invaluable to us! Unfortunately, April 23 was our last night running delivery for this semester. We hope that everybody has a great semester and finishes strong for the summer. To the seniors - we will miss you! Good luck out there. We will be coming back next semester with an even more comprehensive and complete delivery network for Wesleyan.

The FoodStop team wishes everybody at Wesleyan a great summer, and we look forward to working with everybody staying and new students next semester. Thank you.
